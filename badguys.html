Bad Guys
(Ranked from best at their job to worst):

(click images for more information)

  1. Chancellor Palpatine (The Emperor)

    Lurked in the shadows, then became chancellor of the senate as well as being a Sith lord. Kinda dope.

  2. Darth Vader

    Nearly died in lava, but was saved and returned as a Sith Lord, killing a lot of people. Eventually found goodness in his heart and killed his master, Chancellor Palpatine.

  3. Darth Maul

    Main villain in only one movie, but managed to cause a fair amount of chaos before getting chopped in half by Obi-Wan Kenobi

  4. Count Dooku

    Main villain again in only one movie, but it was a worse movie so he's farther down.

  5. Jabba the Hutt

    Kind of a beta villain, only exists as a side plot in two movies, and also does the dumb bad guy thing where he puts the hero in an easily escapable situation and is then surprised when they escape.
